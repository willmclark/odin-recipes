Francis Ford Coppola

This is his tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 medium chopped onion
  • 3 cans of tomatoes, crushed and drained
  • 1 bunch of chopped fresh basil
  • 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 pounds of spaghetti

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan on on medium heat. Saute onions in oil until transparent (about 5-7 minutes). Add tomatoes, 1/4 of the basil and pepper flakes.
  2. Simmer about 45 minutes. Add butter and remaining basil. If sauce is too thick, add some pasta cooking water.
  3. Serve over pasta.
